Pizza

Description

This is a margherita pizza, it's delicious. The cheese is stretchy too

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Mozarella cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough
  2. Prepare the sauce
  3. Put the sauce on the dough
  4. Put the cheese evenly on the top
  5. Cook in oven at 180 degrees celsius for 20 minutes
  6. Voila, you've done it
